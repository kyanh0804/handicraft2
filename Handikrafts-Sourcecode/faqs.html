FAQS

To find information about available items, you can use the search tool at the top of the website or browse through product categories in the navigation bar. Each item will have a detailed page for you to learn more about them.

The store owner's contact information is provided on the website. You can reach out to them directly for assistance and to place an order using the phone number and email address provided.

Each product comes with detailed information, including descriptions, images, prices, and material details. You can click on a product to view its full details.

We offer flexibility for you to place orders for custom products. Please contact the store owner through the provided phone number or email to discuss your specific product requirements.

The inventory status of each product is regularly updated on the website. You can see whether a product is "In Stock" or "Out of Stock" on the product information page. We will also notify you of any changes.

To track the shipping process of your order, you can log in to your account and access the "Track My Order" section or use the order tracking code provided in the order confirmation email. There, you will receive updates on the location and delivery status of your order.
